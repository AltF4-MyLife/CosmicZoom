CosmicZoom by Sky Link is a web platform that transforms massive NASA imagery into an interactive, collaborative exploration space. Inspired by the smooth navigation of Google Maps, it enables anyone to "walk through" gigapixel and terapixel images of Mars, the Moon, and deep space.

CosmicZoom من Sky Link هي منصة ويب تحول صور ناسا الضخمة إلى مساحة استكشاف تفاعلية وتعاونية. مستوحاة من التنقل السلس في خرائط Google، تمكن أي شخص من "المشي خلال" صور بدقة جيجابكسل وتيرابكسل للمريخ والقمر والفضاء العميق.

Project Summary

ملخص الفكرة

CosmicZoom: Space Exploration with Infinite Zoom Technology

CosmicZoom: استكشاف الفضاء بتقنية التكبير اللانهائي

A web platform that transforms NASA's massive images into an interactive exploration experience. Inspired by the ease of browsing in Google Maps, CosmicZoom allows users to explore gigapixel and terapixel images from Mars, the Moon, and deep space.

منصة ويب تحوّل صور ناسا الضخمة إلى تجربة استكشاف تفاعلية. مستوحاة من سهولة التصفح في Google Maps، CosmicZoom تتيح للمستخدمين استكشاف صور بدقة "جيجابكسل" و"تيرابكسل" من المريخ والقمر وأعماق الفضاء.

You can smoothly zoom from a global view to the finest details without performance loss, add notes and areas of interest, and share your discoveries with others.

يمكنك التكبير بسلاسة من عرض كلي إلى أدق التفاصيل بدون فقدان في الأداء، وإضافة ملاحظات ومناطق اهتمام، ومشاركة اكتشافاتك مع الآخرين.

The system includes optional AI assistance to suggest interesting areas, making the exploration process faster and more enjoyable.

يتضمن النظام مساعدة اختيارية من الذكاء الاصطناعي لاقتراح مناطق مثيرة للاهتمام، مما يجعل عملية الاستكشاف أسرع وأكثر متعة.

The Problem & Our Solution

المشكلة وحلنا

The Challenge

التحدي

NASA faces unique challenges in publishing and making its astronomical data available to researchers and the public:

تواجه ناسا تحديات فريدة في نشر وإتاحة بياناتها الفلكية للباحثين والجمهور:

  • Massive File Sizes: NASA's high-resolution images can reach gigapixel or even terapixel sizes, making them difficult to display on standard web browsers or devices. These images require enormous storage space, up to several terabytes per collection.
  • Navigation Complexity: Without proper tools, exploring these detailed images becomes confusing and disorienting. Users easily lose spatial context when zooming in, resulting in a poor user experience.
  • Collaboration Barriers: Researchers struggle to share specific areas of interest within these massive images. Current systems lack effective mechanisms for bookmarking and annotation.
  • Access Gap: The general public lacks intuitive tools to explore and appreciate these amazing resources. Most astronomical data viewers require specialized technical expertise.
  • Discovery Limitations: Manually finding interesting features in vast images is time-consuming and often misses important details. Current systems lack advanced analytical capabilities.
  • Infrastructure Challenges: Hosting and serving this massive data requires sophisticated infrastructure and substantial computing resources, creating barriers for smaller institutions and independent researchers.
  • Update Challenges: NASA faces difficulty in regularly updating datasets while maintaining accessibility, leading to delays in making the latest discoveries available to the scientific community.

Our Solution

CosmicZoom project overcomes these challenges with a modern, user-friendly platform:

  • Efficient Image Processing: Our preprocessing pipeline transforms massive images into optimized deep zoom pyramids that load quickly and smoothly on any device. We use OpenSeadragon technology with custom enhancements to tile images into multi-level tiles.
  • Intuitive Navigation: Inspired by Google Maps, our interface provides familiar zoom and pan tools with contextual awareness of your location within the larger image. Features include a mini-map for orientation and bookmarks for important locations.
  • Collaborative Tools: Researchers can annotate specific regions, bookmark and share them with precise coordinates and custom notes. The system supports real-time collaboration between researchers worldwide.
  • Accessibility for All: A user-friendly interface makes these amazing space images available to everyone, from curious students to professional astronomers. The interface is designed according to WCAG 2.1 standards to ensure inclusive access.
  • AI-Enhanced Discovery: Machine learning algorithms help identify interesting features and suggest areas worth exploring, accelerating scientific discovery. We use Computer Vision models trained on thousands of classified astronomical images.
  • Big Data Storage and Analysis: Scalable infrastructure efficiently handles storage and processing of massive astronomical data. We use Cloud Storage techniques with advanced compression algorithms to reduce storage requirements by up to 60%.
  • Open API: We provide a RESTful API that allows developers and researchers to integrate CosmicZoom data into their own applications and research, promoting open innovation.

Technical Architecture

معالجة البيانات

هذا المكون مسؤول عن معالجة صور ناسا الخام وتجهيزها لمنصة الويب.

  • صور ناسا الخام (جيجابكسل/تيرابكسل) من مصادر مثل NASA Image and Video Library و Planetary Data System.
  • المعالجة المسبقة إلى هرميات التكبير العميق باستخدام أدوات مفتوحة المصدر مثل VIPS و libvips.
  • التكامل مع واجهات برمجة تطبيقات ناسا العامة مثل NASA Open APIs و APOD API.
  • خوارزميات ضغط متقدمة لتقليل حجم البيانات مع الحفاظ على الجودة باستخدام WebP و AVIF.

الخدمة الخلفية والواجهة البرمجية

تقدم الخدمة الخلفية بلاطات الصور المعالجة مسبقًا وتدير تعليقات المستخدمين.

  • خدمة خلفية قائمة على Python باستخدام FastAPI مع Uvicorn كخادم ASGI.
  • تقديم فعال لبلاطات الصور والبيانات التي ينشئها المستخدم مع Redis للتخزين المؤقت.
  • نقاط نهاية واجهة برمجة التطبيقات لإنشاء واسترجاع والبحث في التعليقات التوضيحية باستخدام MongoDB للتخزين.
  • نظام مصادقة آمن باستخدام JWT و OAuth2 للتكامل مع خدمات المصادقة الخارجية.
  • تحليلات متقدمة باستخدام Pandas و NumPy لمعالجة البيانات العلمية.

واجهة المستخدم والعرض

الواجهة الأمامية مسؤولة عن عرض صور التكبير العميق وتوفير تجربة تفاعلية.

  • واجهة ويب حديثة باستخدام React و TypeScript مع Next.js للأداء المحسن.
  • عرض صور التكبير العميق باستخدام OpenSeadragon مع تخصيصات مخصصة للأداء الأمثل.

NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025

The NASA Space Apps 2025 Challenge: 'Embiggen Your Eyes'

The "Embiggen Your Eyes" challenge is one of the most important NASA Space Apps challenges for 2025, focusing on finding innovative solutions to handle massive astronomical data and making it accessible to everyone in an easy and effective way.

NASA faces a significant challenge in managing and displaying the enormous amounts of astronomical images and data collected by space observatories and telescopes such as Hubble, James Webb, and Chandra. This data represents a huge scientific treasure, but its massive size and complexity make it difficult for researchers and the general public to fully benefit from it.

"How can we make the observed treasures of the universe available to everyone? How do we transform complex data into a seamless exploratory experience?"

The challenge invites participants to develop innovative technical solutions that help organize, display, and analyze astronomical data in an interactive and user-friendly way, focusing on improving the user experience and facilitating access to scientific information.

Challenge Objectives:

  • Develop user-friendly interfaces for handling high-resolution astronomical images
  • Create tools for smooth zooming in and out of massive astronomical images
  • Facilitate collaboration between researchers in analyzing astronomical data
  • Improve accessibility of astronomical data for the general public
  • Integrate AI techniques to discover interesting patterns and phenomena

Resources & Tools

Here we present a collection of useful resources and tools for developers, researchers, and those interested in space exploration and astronomical application development. These resources helped us build the CosmicZoom project and we hope they will also help you in your future projects.

NASA APIs & Data

NASA APOD API

NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day API provides a featured astronomical image daily with scientific explanation. Perfect for applications displaying updated astronomical content.

API Images Daily
Planetary Data System (PDS)

The Planetary Data System is NASA's official archive for scientific data from planetary exploration missions. It provides raw and processed data from various space missions.

Data Research Archive
NASA Earthdata Search

Advanced search platform for accessing Earth, climate, and atmospheric data from NASA. Provides APIs for accessing large datasets.

Earth Climate Big Data
Hubble Space Telescope API

Hubble Space Telescope API provides access to thousands of high-resolution images and scientific data from one of the most famous space telescopes.

Hubble Telescope High-Res
